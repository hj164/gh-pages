About Me

Han Jiang: Design Research & Strategy

👋 Hi, I am Han. I research and design the human side of digital transformation.

When I was a curator intern, I uncovered a lot of biased legacy data in the collection database. I created the first spreadsheet at that museum to survey these entries. I translated it into an inclusive research guideline that changed the way other staff used sensitive data. 

This is what I do: I design systems to get people embracing positive change.

I started my PhD when "AI" and "Web 3" became buzzwords. Industries rapidly adopted these technological solutions without considering their implications. These include organisations with traditional business models, where implementing new technology is often considered “challenging”. So far, I have interviewed 9 organisations to identify their challenges. I am developing a workbook that enhances organisational learning on implications and requirements for digital transformation, and to help them make informed decisions that drive success. 

My experience in cultural & creative industries taught me to be people-centred. In 2023, I brought 2 award-winning cultural education apps to life, making art accessible to people with diverse cognition needs. I streamlined innovative workflows in UX teams, integrating gen-AI tools in persona research and prototyping.

I lead research to inform decision making. When I worked as a research assistant, I identified research centre's needs for expanded digital inclusion and strategic collaboration, helping it establish effective partnerships with international stakeholders.

I use interviews, surveys, contextual inquiry, and textual analysis to understand how human beings make meanings in systems around them. I only conduct research when it creates solutions that have social impact. I know how to work with product experts, engineers, and data scientists.

I advocate for digital infrastructure to be collaborative, beyond organisational or national silos. I have lectured at universities and conferences worldwide to help people discover opportunities for collaboration and catalysts for change. 

I love experiments, trust-building, and exchange - they empower us to better dwell in this world.
